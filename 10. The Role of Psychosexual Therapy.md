## What Is Psychosexual Therapy?

Psychosexual therapy is the intersection of psychology and sexuality. It addresses:

- Sexual dysfunction and concerns
- Relationship and intimacy issues
- Sexual identity and orientation
- Trauma and its sexual impacts
- Desire discrepancies
- Communication about sex

Unlike general therapy, it specifically focuses on sexual health and wellbeing. And unlike casual conversation, it's grounded in evidence-based understanding of human sexuality.
